Musicly

Musicly is a simple and elegant open-source music player.

Elegance

In musicly, there is nothing to distract you from your loved music. Just a couple nice looking buttons.

Covers

It is annoying when you can't see your covers. Therefore, covers are first-class citizens in Musicly.

Dark theme & Custom CSS

Are you an obscure hacker, typing 0s and 1s on a monochrome display? We've got something for you!

Mini-player mode

Minimize Musicly to a small window and keep working.

Edit album and track metadata

Changes are automatically saved to the files if they are in mp3 format

Inactivity mode

Musicly detects when you are inactive and displays the album's cover and track info.

Documentation & Contributing

If you are looking to get started with Musicly, you can download it or check out our user documetation, were you will find a complete overview of the program's features.

If you want to help out, I'd love to have someone sharing their knowledge with me. I'm fairly new to React, Electron and even programming itself. All help and tips are welcome! Take a look at Musicly's developer documentation.
